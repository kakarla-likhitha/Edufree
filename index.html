Future Paths After 10th

Explore various career options available after completing 10th grade in India.

1. Science Stream (PCM/PCB/PCMB)

  • PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics): Leads to Engineering, Architecture, Data Science, etc.
  • PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology): Leads to Medicine (MBBS, BDS, BPT, etc.), Pharmacy, Biotech, etc.
  • PCMB (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology): Keeps both engineering & medical options open.

2. Commerce Stream

  • Career paths: Chartered Accountant (CA), Company Secretary (CS), Banking, Business Management (BBA/MBA), Stock Market, etc.

3. Arts/Humanities Stream

  • Career paths: Journalism, Psychology, Law, Civil Services (UPSC), Teaching, Fine Arts, etc.

4. Polytechnic Courses

  • Diplomas in Engineering (Mechanical, Civil, Electrical, etc.)
  • Leads to direct entry into B.Tech or job opportunities.

5. ITI (Industrial Training Institute)

  • Courses in Electrician, Plumbing, Welding, Carpentry, etc.
  • Leads to job opportunities in the industrial sector.

6. Paramedical Courses

  • Diploma in Medical Lab Technology (DMLT)
  • Diploma in Radiology, Physiotherapy, Nursing, etc.

7. Vocational Courses

  • Courses in Photography, Fashion Design, Hotel Management, etc.
  • Leads to job placements in respective industries.

8. Armed Forces (Defense Jobs)

  • Join the Indian Army through the Soldier Entry Scheme.
  • Join the Indian Navy/Air Force through technical entry schemes.
